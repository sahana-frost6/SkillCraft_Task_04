# Traffic Accident Data Analysis (SkillCraft Technology Task 04)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes a countrywide traffic accident dataset to identify patterns related to road conditions, weather, and time of day. It also visualizes accident hotspots across the United States.

## 📊 Key Insights
- **Time of Day:** Accidents peak during morning (7-9 AM) and evening (4-6 PM) rush hours.
- **Weather Conditions:** A significant number of accidents occur during 'Fair' weather, suggesting traffic volume is a higher risk factor than visibility.
- **Road Features:** Intersections and Traffic Signals are the most common locations for recorded incidents.
- **Hotspots:** Accident density is highest in major metropolitan hubs on the East Coast and Southern California.
